Politics

I think my political views would probably be best described as Anarcho-Collectivist. I believe:

  • Ownership and Markets are not necessarily bad things
  • Having a State in charge will inevitably lead to coercion and misrepresentation, regardless form.
  • Certain goods and services should be provided regardless of one's contribution to society (food, water, healthcare, etc.)

I am aware that the existence of an anarchist state is very optimistic, but I believe in it regardless.
